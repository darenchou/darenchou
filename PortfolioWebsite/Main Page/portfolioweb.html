DAREN CHOU'S PORTFOLIO

About Me

My name is Daren Chou, 21 years old. I'm a photographer, photo and video editor, developer, and dancer from San Jose, California.

I am currently studying photography at San Jose State University in my junior year.

I enjoy traveling around the world to experience different settings, cultures, and angles, shooting photos of just about anything. As a photographer-in-training, I try to visualize and capture images of the world through various perspectives!

This portfolio website is a collection of all of my works created over the course of my fine arts studies, each one of them expresses my growth and improvement as an artist and photographer.

Professional Skillset:

Adobe Lightroom, Adobe Photoshop CC, Adobe Premiere Pro CC, Sublime Text (HTML and CSS), P5.js, Sketchfab

My Portfolio

Nature/Architecture of the World Series


Images taken during my travels around the world, depicting the natural and architectural scenes; in some cases maybe both.

Bustling City Series


Using long exposure, these photos were taken when night falls and lights flicker on, in which cityscapes become bright, colorful, and lively environments.

One Man Troupe Series


Challenging my Adobe Photoshop skills, "One Man Troupe" expresses my passion of dance and my desire to take to the stage with fellow dancers again.

Studio Series

Digital Media Series


This series portrays the various digital media and coding projects created in my digital media art course, including a 3D collage and an interactive art piece.

Video Project: Losing It...
